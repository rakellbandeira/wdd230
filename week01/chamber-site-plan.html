Site Plan

Site Name:

Chamber of Commerce

A local organization of businesses that work together to strengthen the business interests of the group. The intent is to foster business networking and to advocate on the behalf of the community and, for our purposes, advertise the attractions of the city.

Site Purpose:

The overall goal of the chamber of commerce site is to promote the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people.

Scenarios:

What resources does the chamber offer to help local businesses grow and succeed?
What are the benefits of joining the chamber, and how much does membership cost?
Where can I find information on the local economy and demographics to inform my business decisions?
Are there any upcoming networking events or workshops that can help me connect with other local businesses?
Where can I learn about the permits or regulations needed to start a business in this area?
