How to Get There?

Fly to Manaus-Eduardo Gomes International Airport (MAO). The following airports offer international direct flights to Manaus:

  1. Panamá Tocumen International Airport via Copa Airlines
  2. Bogotá El Dorado International Airport via Avianca
  3. Fort Lauderdale International Airport via Azul Airlines

Alternatively, you can fly to some of the main Brazilian airports which have daily flights to Manaus, such as Sao Paulo (Guarulhos), Rio de Janeiro (Galeão), Belem or Brasilia.
